Sauna

Our sauna is the perfect place to unwind and detoxify your body. The heat helps to relax your muscles and improve circulation, while the steam clears your pores and promotes healthy skin.

Hamam

Experience the ultimate in relaxation with our Hamam. Our skilled therapists will guide you through a traditional Turkish bath, using warm water and gentle exfoliation to cleanse and rejuvenate your skin.

Finnish sauna

Step into our traditional Finnish sauna and feel the heat penetrate deep into your muscles, helping to release tension and relieve stress. The dry heat also improves circulation.

Infrared sauna

Our infrared sauna uses the power of light to soothe your muscles. The gentle heat is perfect for those who prefer a more moderate sauna experience, and can be especially helpful for those with chronic pain.
